Kolkata, formerly Calcutta, is West Bengal's vibrant capital, known as the "City of Joy" and India's cultural heart. Situated on the Hooghly River, it's a hub of art, literature, and intellectual life, boasting landmarks like Howrah Bridge and Victoria Memorial. Famous for Durga Puja and street food, it's a unique blend of old-world charm and bustling city energy.
